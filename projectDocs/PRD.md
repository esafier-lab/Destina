## 1. Executive Summary  
**Destina** is an AI-powered web app that creates personalized, budget-based itineraries in seconds.  
Users enter their destination, dates, budget, group size, and trip style (fun, cultural, or religious).  
The app suggests food, lodging, and activities, calculates total costs, and lets users edit and track their trip.  
**Problem:** Planning trips is stressful, scattered across websites, and rarely aligned with personal budgets or styles.

|----------|-------------|--------------------|
